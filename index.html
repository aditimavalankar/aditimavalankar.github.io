Aditi Mavalankar

I am a 2nd year PhD student in the Computer Science department at UC San Diego. I completed my undergrad with Honours at IIIT Hyderabad in August 2016.

I spent two wonderful summers (Applied Scientist, 2018 and SDE, 2017) interning at Amazon Lab126 in the Computer Vision team.

I am currently co-organizing ExploreCSR@UCSD, a Google-funded workshop for promoting Computer Science research among women. During my Master's at UC San Diego, I was the graduate student mentor of the Early Research Scholars Program (ERSP), led by Prof. Christine Alvarado, aimed at promoting research among undergraduate students, especially students from communities underrepresented in Computer Science.

Other than my work, I love singing (check out my YouTube channel!), dancing and reading.

Research

I am interested in devising sample-efficient algorithms for learning transferrable representations for skills and tasks using Deep Reinforcement Learning. In particular, I am focusing on composition of value functions to enable combination of and transfer across skills and tasks.
