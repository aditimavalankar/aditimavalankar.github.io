Biography

I am a graduate student in the Computer Science department at UC San Diego. Earlier, I was an undergraduate student at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H) and got my B.Tech. in CSE in August 2016. My research interests include deep learning, artificial intelligence and automated reasoning.

I love singing (check out my YouTube channel), dancing and reading. I also recently started liking adventure sports!
